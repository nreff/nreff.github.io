\question
Let $T= \left[
\begin{array}{ccccc}
1 & 3 & -1 & 2 & 1 \\
-2 & -6 & 3 & 4 & 2
\end{array} \right]$

Suppose that $T$ is used to define a function in the usual way.

\begin{parts}
\part Compute the rank of $T$.
\part What is the domain of $T$?
\part What is the codomain of $T$?
\part Is $T$ onto? Why or why not?
\part Is $T$ one-to-one? Why or why not?
\part Is $T$ a one-to-one correspondence? Why or why not?

\vspace{1pc}
\end{parts}
